Click a node's employee count to expand or collapse its "child" node(s), ie the employee's immediate subordinates. A node without an employee count has no "child" employees.

Click a node to "drill into" the node, ie that clicked node becomes the new "root" (top level) node. The "breadcrumb" updates to display his/her manager(s).

Breadcrumb names are links that, like clicking a node, drill into the clicked name's node.
